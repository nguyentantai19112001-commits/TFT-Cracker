## Economy & Leveling

1. **Maximize interest.** Every 10g up to 50 grants +1g interest (max +5g). Aim to end rounds at ~50g or just above each 10g threshold (32, 42, 50) when slow-rolling.
2. **Lose-streak gold (NEW in Space Gods).** +1g at 2–4 losses, +2g at 5, +3g at 6+. Combines with interest (e.g. 50g + 6-loss = 58g income).
3. **Win-streak bonus.** +1g at 2–4 wins, +2g at 5, +3g at 6+. Extend streaks when ahead.
4. **XP costs (Set 17).** 7→8 = 36 XP, 8→9 = 28 XP, 9→10 = 68 XP. 8→9 was lowered from 76, making Level 9 competitive with staying at 8.
5. **Shop odds at Level 7.** 19% 2-cost, 30% 3-cost, 40% 4-cost, 10% 5-cost. (Patch 17.1: 2-cost +3%, 3-cost −3%.)

## Realm of the Gods (carousel replacement)

6. **Realm of the Gods offerings.** Each carousel slot, choose between two Space Gods' rewards (each with a guaranteed component). On 4-7, your aligned god gives an Armory boon. Lower-HP players get higher-cost units from Pengu (anvils instead of random components).
7. **Kayle's Radiant boon.** Kayle's God upgrades one completed item on your board to Radiant (doubled stats). Save Reforger augments for item conversion; Prismatic augment can yield two Radiants.
13. **God-pick strategy by rank/style.** Ahri for econ/XP. Soraka for HP (lose-streak oriented). Varus/Ekko for unit copies (hyper-roll). Evelynn for high immediate value (HP tradeoff).
39. **Position for chosen god.** Evelynn (loss) → play aggressive/risky. Ahri (gain) → scale long. Pengu high-cost offer → reserve board slots for those units.

## Traits

14. **Key trait summaries (verify via CD):** Space Groove (AS buff from shields), Primordian (spawn Swarmlings on damage, drop 1/2-cost units), Dark Star (team ability burst at 90% HP).
15. **Primordian synergy.** 3+ Primordians: kill grants gold, damage events spawn Swarmlings. Run ≥2 Primordians (Bel'Veth, Rek'Sai, Briar).
16. **Meeple synergy.** 7 Meeples: one evolves into Mega Meeple with AoE heal/shield amplification.
17. **N.O.V.A. synergy.** 5 N.O.V.A.: allies fire rockets on takedowns (Nova Strike). Prioritize fast-firing carries.
18. **Timebreaker synergy.** (2) +2 MP5 on win, (4) +10% AS on win. Use in win-streak fast builds.
19. **Astronaut synergy.** Gains free Augment at 5★ XP. Example comp: Turbo Doomer (Blitzcrank Party Animal).
20. **Bastion synergy.** Shields + frontline utility. Jax, Shen, Taric, Ornn.
21. **Dark Lady synergy.** Vex's unique trait. Ability tethers + strikes. Backline mage carry with Sorcerer items.
22. **Conduit synergy.** 3 Conduits = small ally AP; 5 = large ally AP. Stack for AP carries (Sona/Viktor).

## Scouting & Adaptation

8. **Scout for contested units.** Watch for 3-cost carries (Fizz, Kai'Sa, Ornn) and 4-costs contested. Pivot early if many players target yours.
40. **Scout + adapt items.** Note enemy carries/items before PvP. If your intended 3★ is contested, pivot. If build mismatches (IE/Crit vs all-tank lobby) convert to anti-tank items.
44. **Plan pivots.** Keep 2 comp plans. Kayle offer → favor item-scaling fast-8/fast-9 over reroll. Use augments like Backup Plans / Bench Leads as pivot flags.
46. **Counter common comps.**
    - Primordian (Swarm-Storm): %HP shred / healing reduction.
    - Snipers (Xayah/Jhin): front-load MR.
    - AoE mages (Viktor/Nunu): scatter carries, Bastion for stuns.
    - N.O.V.A.: high-HP tanks + tenacity.

## Roll / Level Timing

9. **Hyper-roll vs slow-roll decision.** 1-cost reroll: hyper-roll at Level 5–6. 2-cost reroll: hyper-roll 5–6. 3-cost reroll: slow-roll at Level 7 (e.g. "Rogue Diff"). Use augments as signals.
10. **Spike rounds by archetype.**
    - 1-cost reroll: 3-1, 3-5, 4-1
    - 2-cost reroll: 3-2, 4-1, 4-5
    - 3-cost reroll: 3-2, 3-5, 4-1, 5-1
    - Fast-8: 4-2
11. **Leveling on streak.** Win-streak: 7 by 3-1, 8 by 4-2, 9 by 5-2. Lose-streak: hold 6–7 through stage 3, level to 9 only after stability (ideally 4-5).
12. **Economic tempo stage 2–3.** Lv4 by 2-2 if possible, Lv5 by 3-2, roll/slow-roll on 4-2 (BunnyMuffins). Vertical (1-trait) boards if uncertain.
41. **Hold level on reroll until 3★.** 1-cost reroll: do NOT press level until 3★ achieved. 2/3-cost: level to 6 on 3-2 then light slow-roll. L7 only after 3-cost stabilized (4-1 or 4-2).
42. **Interest thresholds ("Vacay" stops).** 32g on 3-1 + no pressure → hit 40g on 3-2 for +4g interest. Avoid dropping below thresholds unnecessarily.
43. **Hyper-roll timing.** Finish 3★s by 4-5. Roll after last PvP before Stage 4 (3-5 or 4-1) to stabilize. Slow-roll only if survival-critical after 4-5.

## Late Game

45. **Carry augment scaling.** Reroll comp: Inner Fire (AP boost Kayle reroll). Prismatic: Golden Idol for triple-cast damage on Rogue. Match augments to build path.
47. **Augment prioritization.**
    - Early reroll: Sec (upgrade unit), Big Brain (dupes) with Varus/God.
    - Early hyper-roll: Brute Force, Fat Loot (free items).
    - Fast-9: Give Me More, Star Trail (XP).
    - Late: Hyperroll (1-cost comps), Legendaries/Heirloom (high-cost).
49. **Check HP + streak each PvP.** Low HP approaching 4-7 → align with loss-streak God or Pengu 3-cost offer. High HP → win-streak god (Soraka/Evelynn). Post-4-7 boons drive late-game gear.
50. **Endgame checklist (7-1 onward).**
    1. Main carry 2★/3★ with 2 strong items.
    2. Re-scout before 7-1; adjust front/back.
    3. HP lead → riskier augments or drop unit for econ.
    4. HP behind → max carry output (full econ augment or single-shot reroll).
    5. Keep key buffs in range (Astronaut carriers near Orbitum).
    6. Final carousel 4-7: pick Armory vs second God for carry class.
